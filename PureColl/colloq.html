Programme of the Pure Mathematics Colloquium

The colloquium takes place on Thursdays at 4pm in Theatre B of the Mathematical Institute (if not indicated otherwise).

Oct. 2nd (Lect. Th. B) M. Albert (Univ. of Otago, New Zealand) Simple permutations
Oct. 9th (Room 1A) G. Walsh (Univ. of Ottawa) On the arithmetical structure of terms in Lucas sequences
Oct. 16th (Room 1A) E. Dombi (Univ. St. Andrews) HNN extensions of inverse semigroups
Oct. 22nd J. Toland (Univ. of Bath) Monotonicity and linking in the calculus of variations
Nov. 5th (Room 1A) I. Gordon (Univ. of Glasgow) New results on the invariant theory of finite reflection groups
Nov. 27th S. Cohen (Univ. of Glasgow) Generators of finite fields: complete answers to existence questions
Feb. 26th L. Davison (Edinburgh and Laurentian) Recent results in the transcendence of the b-ary and the continued fraction expansions of sequences of positive integers with small block-complexity.
Mar. 4th L. Ephremitze (Univ. of Tibilisi) On ergodic maximal functions
Mar. 11th M. Quick (Univ St Andrews) Probabilistic generation of profinite groups
Apr. 1st D. Holt (Warwick Univ.) The word and conjugacy problems in hyperbolic groups.
Apr. 8th S. Murray (Univ. Eindhoven, Univ. Sydney) Computing in groups of Lie type.
May 27th L. Wisewell (Univ. Edinburgh) Oscillatory Integrals and Curved Kakeya Sets.
