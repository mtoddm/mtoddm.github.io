Scottish Dynamics Network

This network connects people working in topics around dynamical systems in Scotland and is funded by the Edinburgh Mathematical Society and the Glasgow Mathematical Journal Trust. There will be an afternoon-long meeting at each of the Universities of Glasgow, Heriot Watt and St Andrews through the academic year 2024-25. Details of these will be announced as they become available.

Glasgow meeting, Friday 20th September

Further details to be announced, but the plan is that the meeting will take place in the Maths Dept at Glasgow in the afternoon.
