Registration costs £80, with a discounted rate of £50 for PhD students (early bird rates were available up to 10th April). Registration is now closed. Email for enquiries.

The conference dinner will take place in Lower College Hall on the evening of Wednesday 13th June, 2018 and will cost £42. There are limited places, and you should book your place when you register (if you do not wish to attend simply click "continue" without ticking the dinner option on the relevant page of the registration process).


There will be speed talks: please write to if you are interested in giving one of these. (These are each likely to be between 3 and 7 minutes long.)
